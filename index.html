About Plants

FUN FACTS ON PLANTS

  • We human beings use more than 2000 different types of plants to create various delicious food items in our meals.

  • Caffeine works as a pesticide in a coffee plant.

  • An average size tree can provide enough wood to make 170,100 pencils.

  • Cabbage has 91% water content.

  • Oak trees do not produce acorns until they become 50 years old.

  • A cucumber is not a vegetable, it is a fruit, as it has seeds

IMPORTANCE OF PLANTS

  • More than 20% of the world’s oxygen supply is produced by the Amazon Rainforest.

  • Water travels in the upward direction from the roots to its stem and then into the plant leaves

  • 96% of raw cucumber and 84% of a raw apple is water.

  • Strawberry is the only fruit that has seeds on the outside. It has about an average of 200 seeds.

  • The drug Quinine is obtained from the dried bark of a South-American native tree.
